# Welcome to devrel.directory

This is your go-to resource for all things Developer Relations (DevRel). Whether you're new to the field or a seasoned professional, you'll find valuable information and insights here.

## What is Developer Relations?

Developer Relations is the practice of building and maintaining relationships between a company and its developer community. It's a crucial role that bridges the gap between developers and the products or services they use.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Fundamentals**: Core concepts and principles of DevRel
- **Best Practices**: Tried-and-true strategies for success
- **Tools and Resources**: Curated list of helpful tools for DevRel professionals
- **Case Studies**: Real-world examples of successful DevRel initiatives
- **Community**: Connect with other DevRel professionals
